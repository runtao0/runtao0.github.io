Welcome to Runtao's Game of Life

This is a 3D implementation of Conway's Game of Life with 2333 rules.
This grid is 100x100 and each voxel represents a live cell.
Click on the grid to make a pattern,
Or choose from the preset list in the right and augment.
Press SHIFT and click to remove a block,
Press Start! see life grow!
Once you begin you can,
1. Pause!
2. Use the mouse to explore:
left click to change viewing angle,
middle scroll for zoom,
and right click to change position
