Welcome to Runtao's Game of Life

This is a 3D implementation of Conway's Game of Life
This grid is 100x100, and each voxel represents a live cell.
Click on the grid to add cells and make a pattern,
Or choose a preset pattern in the middle
Press SHIFT and click to remove a block,
Click Start see life grow!


Once you begin you can explore using the mouse
left/single click to change viewing angle, middle scroll for zoom, right/double click to change position
